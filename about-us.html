Welcome to SheThrives

SheThrives Africa leverages on technology to provide business development training, mentorship and support to women startups, to enable them bring their business ideas to life. We create a community where women collaborate, learn and THRIVE! together.

Women are avid entrepreneurs and their contribution to the economy is enormous. However, many of them lack the required skill and support to lift their businesses from idea to launch. Being a woman-owned business, SheThrives is fully devoted to training and mentoring women entrepreneurs in tech to realise their dreams without compromising the quality of their lives.

Non-tech businesses have also beneﬁted from us immensely, and some of them have ended up, using tech to support their solutions.

Our Mission

Our mission is to support women entrepreneurs to live a life of signiﬁcance by providing infrastructure, business development and support.

Our Vision

Our vision is to have all women transition from dependence to signiﬁcance. Where women live a THRIVING! life, not just having a sustainable livelihood. To attain a gender equality where women are ﬁnancially independent and radically successful.
